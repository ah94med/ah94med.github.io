Locale

Website . CMS

Project Overview

Locale is a Sudanese space for the development and support of a home-grown creative effort. locale website needed to reflect the platform culture with focus on highlighting what the platform is about and the projects that were carried out, a minimal design approach was taken focusing more on accessibility.

Role

Website Co-design Development Consultant

The Challenge

The main challenge we met when developing and structuring the website was the projects pages, the projects pages needed to accommodate different layouts as every project will have different content while also give the admins the ability to upload the projects.

The Solution

The solution was to create repeatable CMS fields like, a repeatable video field, a repeatable picture filed...etc, and an on/off switches to the standard elements like the hero-cover of the projects as some projects do not have covers or media.
