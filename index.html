process

UNDERSTANDING

1.Empathizing

It’s very important to understand our user’s needs and emotions first before implementing.

2.Defining

use the research conducted to define what problems we are trying to solve for our users.

EXPLORING

3.Ideating

at this stage we challenge assumptions, think outside the box and brainstorm ideas to solve the problems we defined.

4.Prototyping

after decide on those great ideas we came up with, a prototype is created to experiment with those ideas.

MATERIALIZING

5.Testing

with our nice prototype ready, it’s time to test it with our users and get any feedback to help us in change or inspire new ideas.

6.Implementation

after the feedback and reassessment of our ideas, it’s time for finalization and implementation of the solution.
