Locale.

Locale is a Sudanese space for the development and support of a home-grown creative effort. We work to challenge the creative community by promoting exploration with the aim of empowering the Sudanese identity and preserving the culture. locale website needed to reflect the platform culture with focus on highlighting what the platform is about and the projects that were carried out, a minimal design approach was taken focusing more on accessibility, the website was built using OctoberCMS in the backed bootstrap and jQuery on the frontend.
